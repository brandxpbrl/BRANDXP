# STRATEGY AGENT - WORKFLOW

## Operational Flow

### 1. Orient

Identify:

- business context
- current stage
- audience
- offer
- goal
- available signals

---

### 2. Diagnose

Analyze:

- strategic gap
- positioning weakness
- offer confusion
- audience mismatch
- growth bottleneck
- perception risk

---

### 3. Prioritize

Define:

- highest leverage decision
- urgent correction
- medium-term opportunity
- what should not be done yet

---

### 4. Architect

Build:

- positioning direction
- roadmap
- offer implication
- customer journey implication
- content implication
- experience implication

---

### 5. Activate

Convert strategy into:

- concrete next action
- deliverable to create
- decision to validate
- execution sequence
