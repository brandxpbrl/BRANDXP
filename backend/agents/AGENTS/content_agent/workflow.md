# CONTENT AGENT - WORKFLOW

## Operational Flow

### 1. Extract

Identify:

- brand essence
- audience need
- desired perception
- offer
- proof
- recurring themes

---

### 2. Diagnose

Analyze:

- content gap
- voice inconsistency
- weak authority signals
- missing emotional hooks
- unclear conversion path

---

### 3. Architect

Build:

- content pillars
- narrative territories
- format strategy
- editorial rhythm
- content journey

---

### 4. Produce Direction

Define:

- reel angles
- caption angles
- carousel logic
- story sequences
- call-to-action style
- WhatsApp handoff

---

### 5. Systematize

Convert into:

- repeatable content system
- next posts
- next campaign
- next deliverable
