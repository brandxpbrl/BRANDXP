# Brand Identity Board - Mau Viagens Tours

## Tagline

La marca tiene una base aprovechable; el mayor valor esta en convertir senales en sistema.

## Brand Descriptor

Las fuentes muestran senales utiles, pero el sistema debe separar identidad, percepcion visual, narrativa y conversion para operar con precision.

## Logo System

- Primary: Use current primary logo as the main recognition asset.
- Variants: Horizontal lockup, Compact lockup, Single-color version
- Icon: Use or derive a simplified symbol from the most recognizable brand mark.

## Palette

- **Signature Accent:** Emotional distinction and premium attention.
- **Deep Base:** Depth, contrast, and editorial focus.
- **Clarity Light:** Legibility, simplicity, and trust.

## Typography

- Primary: High-contrast editorial sans
- Secondary: Clean supporting sans
- Usage: Use strong hierarchy for titles and restrained supporting text for details.

## Brand Tone

- **Diferenciacion:** Convertir atributos genericos en codigos propietarios: rituales, lenguaje, visuales y experiencia.
- **Premium confidence:** Sound curated, specific, and operational rather than generic.

## Applications

- **Digital presence:** Apply the identity consistently across website, social profile, and sales material.
- **Client proposal:** Translate the identity into offer pages, highlights, and proof points.

## Instagram Style

- **Autoridad:** Demostrar criterio, metodo y dominio de la categoria.
- **Deseo:** Convertir el destino o servicio en una experiencia deseable, no solo en itinerario.
- **Prueba:** Reducir incertidumbre con evidencia, casos, testimonios y proceso.
- **Conversion:** Guiar al publico hacia consulta, reserva, compra o diagnostico.

## Commercial Example

- Offer: Premium brand experience package
- Services: Diagnosis, Identity direction, Content system, Execution roadmap
- CTA: Book a strategic discovery session.

## Benefits

- **Sharper perception:** Help the audience understand the value faster.
- **Reusable system:** Turn scattered ideas into rules that can guide future assets.
